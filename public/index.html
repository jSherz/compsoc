Leeds Uni CompSoc Gaming

Who are we?

CompSoc is back with a bang! So when you get over-worked, come and blow off some steam and get involved with nights out, gaming, bowling and much, much more!

We’ve got a whole host of activities planned including Otley runs, LAN parties and some big nights out as part of the Faculty of Engineering so bring your friends and come and get involved!

CompSoc is a society for like minded people, and open to everyone – not just the School of Computing. Keep up to date with the current goings-on through our website, our Facebook group and Twitter feed!

If you've just started at Leeds Uni and you're not yet a member, then don't miss out - join CompSoc today!
